    • About Us

    TURKANA TECH YOUTH HUB is a social enterprise dedicated to transforming the lives of youth through hands-on skill training, with a mission to eliminate poverty, combat drug abuse, reduce gender-based violence, address insecurities, and, most importantly, tackle climate change.

    Our Aim:

    We strive to empower youth with TVET skills, fostering economic growth and promoting a more sustainable, equitable, and safe society through innovative technological solutions.

    Mission:

    Our mission is to empower the youth of Turkana through quality Technical and Vocational Education and Training, climate resilience initiatives, fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and employability to drive community growth and improve livelihoods.

    Vision:

    To create a self-reliant and skilled youth community in Turkana, driving sustainable development and socio-economic transformation through the power of Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET)

    Our Core Values:

    • Empowerment:
      Committing to empowering youths by providing access to skills and opportunities that foster self-reliance and personal growth.


    • Community-Centered:
      Prioritizing the needs of the community, ensuring that programs and initiatives align with local aspirations and contribute to collective well-being..


    • Innovation:
      Encouraging creativity and innovation in TVET programs, enabling youth to develop new solutions and approaches to local challenges.


    • Inclusivity:
      Ensuring that all youth, regardless of background or circumstances, have equal access to opportunities and resources.


    • Sustainability:
      Promoting practices and projects that are environmentally and economically sustainable, ensuring long-term benefits for the community.


    • Collaboration:
      Fostering partnerships with local and external stakeholders to enhance the reach and impact of TVET initiatives.


    • Integrity:
      Upholding the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and accountability in all activities and interactions.
